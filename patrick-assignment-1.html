Tested on Brain and Behavior

Scientist at Brain.fm work with collaborators at academic institutions to run experiments.

We look at the effects of our technology on the brain, using fMRI and EEG, and also run large-scale behavorial tests measuring performance on simple games

Our experiments always include a control condition ('placebo') of the same underlying music without Brain.fm technology applied, so we can make sure our tech is what makes the difference.
